TabSweep Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 3, 2026

Summary

TabSweep is designed to work locally in your browser. It evaluates page URLs against the regex patterns you provide and closes tabs that match. No browsing data, patterns, or settings are sent to the developer or any third party.

Information We Store

  • Regex patterns and settings (mode, idle delay, “close on click”): stored using Chrome/Edge storage.sync so they can follow you across signed-in browsers.
  • No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored by the developer.

How Information Is Used

Your patterns are used solely to test against the URLs of tabs open in your browser so TabSweep can decide whether to close them. The extension does not log, export, or share these URLs.

Permissions

  • tabs: Needed to read tab URLs and close tabs that match your patterns.
  • storage: Needed to save your patterns and preferences.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. No analytics, ads, or tracking libraries are included.

Security

Data is stored using the browser’s built-in extension storage. Because nothing is transmitted to our servers, risk is limited to your local device and browser account. Keep your device secure and sign out of browsers on shared machines.

Children’s Privacy

TabSweep is a utility extension and does not target children. It does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be reflected in this document. The “Effective date” will update when revisions are published.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, please use the support contact provided on the Chrome Web Store or Microsoft Edge Add-ons listing.
