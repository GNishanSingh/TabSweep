TabSweep

Regex-powered tab cleaner for Chrome and Edge.

Why

  • Auto-close tabs whose URLs match your regex list.
  • Immediate or idle-close modes; optional “close on click.”
  • Pinned/non-HTTP(S) tabs are always safe.

Safety

  • Patterns live in storage.sync; nothing leaves your browser.
  • Live regex validation blocks saves until patterns compile.
  • No analytics, ads, or tracking libraries.

How to use

  • Open Options → add one regex per line.
  • Choose close mode and idle delay.
  • Hit “Run now” in the popup to scan existing tabs.

Privacy

Effective: February 3, 2026

  • Stores only your regex patterns and settings in storage.sync to keep them in your signed-in browsers.
  • Does not collect, transmit, or sell browsing data; no analytics or ads.
  • Permissions: tabs (read/close matching URLs) and storage (save preferences).
  • Data stays local to the browser; keep your device and profile secure on shared machines.
