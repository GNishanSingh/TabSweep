TabSweep

Provide one JavaScript regular expression per line. Tabs whose URL matches any regex will close automatically. Regex mistakes are highlighted so you can fix them before saving.

Closing behavior

In idle mode, matching tabs close only after the delay and only if they are not the active tab.

Tips

  • Regex is case-sensitive unless you add (?i) or i flags inside your pattern.
  • Non-http(s) pages and pinned tabs are never auto-closed.
  • Keep patterns narrow to avoid closing tabs you need.
